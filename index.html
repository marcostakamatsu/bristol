Industries We Serve

Specialized fastening solutions for the projects that build and power Southern California — from utility-scale solar arrays to high-rise curtain wall and the structural steel that holds them together.

  • Alternative Energy & Utilities

    Stainless and high-grade fasteners stocked for ground-mount and rooftop solar, wind installations, and utility infrastructure.

  • Curtain Wall

    Anchor systems and concealed fasteners specified to architectural tolerances and supplied to facade-installation schedules.

  • Industrial Fasteners

    MRO-grade fasteners for plants, mills, and process facilities. Consistent supply for the inventories that can't run dry.

  • Marine Fasteners

    Corrosion-resistant fasteners and assemblies built for dock, vessel, and shoreline applications in coastal environments.

  • Structural Steel Fastener Systems

    F1554 anchor bolts, A325 and A490 structural bolts, and high-strength assemblies for the connections that hold buildings up.
